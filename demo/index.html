svg.draw.js - Demo

Rectangle, click to draw

new SVG('rect').size('100%', '100%') .rect().draw()

Polygon, press Enter to finish the shape

new SVG('polygon').size('100%', '100%') .polygon().draw();

Ellipse, click to draw

new SVG('circle').size('100%', '100%') .circle().draw();

Circle, click to draw

new SVG('circleUseRadius').size('100%', '100%') .circle().draw({useRadius:true})

Polygon, aligned at grig

new SVG('polygonGrid').size('100%', '100%') .polygon().draw({snapToGrid:20})

Rectangle, mousedown/move/up to draw

var rect = new SVG('rectNoClick') .size('100%', '100%').rect(); document.addEventListener('mousedown', function(e){ rect.draw(e); }, false); document.addEventListener('mouseup', function(e){ rect.draw(e); }, false);
